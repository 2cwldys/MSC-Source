*Server*

CBasePlayer
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
iuser1 - sent to the client for player ID
iuser4 - sent to the server (via cliend cmd: "attack X") Indicates which 
		 attack the client picked

CGenericItem
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

		 this to determine if they should die this frame.

CMSMonster
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
